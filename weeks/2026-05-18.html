Switching activity

🚨 BusyBook: New Direct Competitor

BusyBook (busybook.co) launched BusyBook for Massage in March 2026, explicitly targeting solo massage therapists as the primary ICP. Their SEO content is already ranking #1 for "Vagaro alternative massage therapist." Product positioning mirrors Roomwell's target opportunity:

  • Pricing: Free / $20 Starter / $100 Pro/mo — no contracts, no add-ons
  • Differentiation: AI-powered SOAP notes (Starter+), AI front desk — client communication via text 24/7 (Pro)
  • Target: Solo LMTs switching from Vagaro — explicitly named
  • URL: busybook.co/blog/vagaro-alternative-massage-therapists/

BusyBook is the first confirmed, funded, named competitor in Roomwell's exact product space. Their Starter ($20/mo) prices directly against ClinicSense and MassageBook. Track their feature velocity and content output weekly from this point.

Vagaro → Mangomint (confirmed pattern)

"My spa used vagaro for a full year and we couldn't wait to switch. While Vagaro offers many features, they up charge for everything and their software is not reliable. The constant bugs with the platform made us eventually switch to Mangomint which we have been very happy with!"Valentina A., Dec 2025 — Capterra (follow-up)

Second reviewer also named Mangomint as destination: John G., Dec 2025. Mangomint is ~$165+/mo — this is an upmarket cohort (multi-provider wellness), not Roomwell's solo LMT ICP, but confirms churn is real.

Mindbody → Vagaro (mid-journey cohort)

Switched TO Vagaro FROM Mindbody — showing the migration chain. Some Vagaro users are Mindbody refugees who haven't found their final home.
